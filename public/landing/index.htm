Features

Broadcast your Event's Picz

Stream live photos and videos tagged on Facebook, Instagram & Twitter during your event. Display on large screens a giant slideshow or a mosaic full of magical and memorable photos.

#Hashtag Aggregation

Choose your hashtag and we will aggregate public user generated content in real time to build your own feed.

Entertain & Engage

Inspire your attendees to take & share more photos with our live slideshow visualization. Everything works in realtime, content will appear within your stream in a few seconds.

Share your Event

Propagate your live stream online though our web widget, it will work on any device and your event will reach a wider audience. Leverage the momentum!

Curated Content

Our advanced tools will help you to automatically avoid unsafe content. Keep control of your stream!

Brand Design

Use your own design to fit your brand. Choose between our elegant designs or create your 100% personalized template. In few minutes upload your own logo, background and banners.
